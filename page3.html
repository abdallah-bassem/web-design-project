How can I request a security assessment?

You can request a security assessment by filling out the contact form on our website, emailing us directly at contact@Z3r0d4y.com, or calling our office. Our team will respond promptly to discuss your requirements and provide a detailed proposal based on your organization's specific needs.

How long does a typical security assessment take?

The duration of a security assessment varies depending on the scope and complexity of your environment. A basic assessment might take 1-2 weeks, while a comprehensive evaluation of a complex infrastructure could take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you provide post-assessment support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-assessment support services. This includes remediation guidance, verification testing, and ongoing security monitoring. We can also provide training for your team to help them understand the vulnerabilities identified and how to address them effectively.

How does Z3r0 d4y integrate AI with cybersecurity?

Our AI solutions are designed to enhance traditional security measures by identifying patterns and threats that might otherwise go undetected.
